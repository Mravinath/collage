Hoyasan labs is a MedTech startup incubated at CPDMeD launched in 2021. We are a deep tech focused company on bringing deep tech innovation to our consumer.
Our Company is a blend of technologists and patriots,who believe in contributing to the country by producing indigenous Pharmaceutical products. We have a vision of making India a self-reliant country in terms of Active pharmaceutical ingredients,which will boost the supply chain in the country. We want to solve this problem and give our best in terms of quality and availability to a larger extent.
Mission to build strong research and design core for building our products. To go above and beyond to solve the problems one at a time.
Hoyasan word is derived from Hoysala empire & current city of Hassan situated in the state of Karnataka.Taking inspiration from the great Hoysala empire for the perfection and elegance of the temple carved in the 10th century which has left a mark for generations. We at Hoyasan labs aim to bring perfection, elegance in our product & intend to leave a mark of our own.
